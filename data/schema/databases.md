# Database schemas

## NH3 Storage

Required sheet: `All_Proposed_Records`

Core fields used by the agent:

- material before NH3 absorption;
- material after NH3 absorption;
- material family/type;
- reported material state;
- phase-change description;
- temperature and NH3 pressure;
- method;
- DOI;
- record type and confidence, when available.

## DigBat

Required sheet: `Sheet1`

Core fields:

- `Pretty_Formula`;
- `Ion_Conductivity`;
- `T_Kelvin`;
- `DOI`.

## DigHyd

Required sheet: `Sheet1`

Core fields:

- material before/after hydrogen absorption;
- gravimetric hydrogen density or release text;
- dehydrogenation temperature and conditions;
- DOI.
